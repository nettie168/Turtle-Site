Turtles

Turtles include sea-dwellers and land-dwellers. Tortoises live on land, terrapins in fresh-water and sea turtles, you guessed it, in the sea.

In Britain, sea turtles, tortoises and terrapins are considered as distinct groups.

There are different species of sea turtles including the leatherback turtles.

Leatherback turtles eat jellyfish. However in the ocean plastic bags look very similar.

These turtles have been found with plastic bags inside them because they mistook them for food
